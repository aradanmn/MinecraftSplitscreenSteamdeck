## The arc

### 1. Root cause pinned
override_redirect only ever won for the FIRST window; then KWin's own `frameGeometry`
ALSO couldn't move the windows — because OUR app had made them all `override_redirect`
(unmanaged). Removing OR made them managed, but they snapped back on every resize.

### 2. Deep-research × 2
- KWin/Plasma 6: managed XWayland windows DO accept `frameGeometry` (synchronous X11
  authority); async "client ignores size" failures are native-Wayland only.
- THE MOD: it's **pcal43/splitscreen "Splitscreen Support"** (or fork FlyingEwok). From its
  Java source: `glfwSetWindowMonitor` + toggles `GLFW_DECORATED`, reads
  `splitscreen.properties` ONCE at init, and **re-asserts its window geometry on every
  resize** → it owns the window and snaps it back. THE reverter. → REMOVE the mod, let KWin
  own windowing. Both research rounds converged.

### 3. Confirmed on screen
Disabled the jar. `test 8` (single sweep): window moved+resized through all 7 positions and
STAYED. `test 4` (full): borderless + quad tiling + scale-down (4→3→2 halves→1 fullscreen) +
clean exit to Steam — user-confirmed. Architecture works.
